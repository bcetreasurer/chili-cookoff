Chili Cook‑Off & Family Night

Saturday, November 15, 2025 · 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM · Boulder Creek Elementary

Join us for the annual Boulder Creek Elementary Chili Cook‑Off! Bring family and friends for a warm night of competition, tasty chilis, kids' activities, raffle prizes, and community fun. All proceeds benefit the school PTA.

Event Schedule
  • 4:30 PM — Check‑in for Chili contestants
  • 5:00 PM — Doors open to families
  • 6:30 PM — Winners announced
  • 7:30 PM — Raffle and closing
Who should come?

Everyone! Families, neighbors, teachers, and anyone who loves chili. We’ll have a kids' corner with crafts and games.

Event FAQs

Where: School cafeteria & gym. Parking: Lot and street parking.

Questions? Contact the PTA at pta@bouldercreek.example

Rules for Chili Contestants
  1. Register in advance and check in by 4:30 PM.
  2. Bring at least 2 gallons of chili in a labeled, sealed container.
  3. No commercial pre-packaged chili; home-made only.
  4. Provide a short recipe card for ingredients (for allergy awareness).
